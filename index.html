Shadowbound

Shadowbound is a couch co-op PvP fighter game packed full of abilities and chaos!

Fight up to 3 of your friends in the Shadow Arena to reclaim the fallen throne and become the one true Shadow King! Choose from 4 unique characters, each belonging to their own class: Tank, Mage, Assassin, and Builder! All 4 have their own unique abilities that can be expanded with a variety of extra skills.

To win, you must be the first to reach the point threshold. For this, you must slay your opponents or be the last man standing in a showdown. Don’t worry—losing gives you perks to help you climb the ranks again.

Main Mechanic: Shadows! As a Prince of Darkness, you aren’t meant to withstand the sun, so you must stay in the shadows at all times. These can be either the permanent shadows cast by buildings or the temporary shadows you create with your abilities. Staying in the sun will result in your character burning to nothing more than a pile of ashes...

So what are you waiting for? Fight for your future as the Shadow King and return glory to the Shadow Kingdom!
AGAINST THE DARKNESS

One fuels the dying furnace, the other fends off the relentless swarm that quickly approaches. In this survival co-op experience, communication and strategy are your only weapons. Will you reach the surface, or succumb to the horrors that lurk in the depths? Against the Darkness awaits. See if you have what it takes.

Features:
  • Intense Co-op Gameplay: Work together to survive. Communication and strategy are key as you manage resources and fend off the ever coming horror.
  • Permadeath: Death is a chilling reality. If one of you dies, the game is over and you have to restart.
  • Quick-paced Gameplay: No matter on what side you start, there is a constant threat keeping up the pressure. If you're not fast enough you will end up as spider food.

    • Please note: This is a work in progress. Features and mechanics may change throughout development.
    Speed for Need

    In this exhilarating driving simulation, you are tasked with controlling an unstoppable car while attempting to evade the police.

    One major challenge is that the car cannot brake, and its parts continually deteriorate and require constant repairs. The vehicle becomes increasingly difficult to handle as it sustains damage.

    Your objectives are to:
    • Escape the police.
    • Prevent your car from breaking down.
    • Maintain control of your vehicle.
    • Cause chaos throughout the town!
    Controls:
    • Press A to steer left.
    • Press D to steer right.

    This game was realized in 3 days for the Unwrap 2024 Game Jam at Howest Digital Arts and Entertainment.
